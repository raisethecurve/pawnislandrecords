High Ground

Urban hip hop / New England identity

High Ground carries the clearest hip hop voice on the label, pairing direct writing with the feel of northeastern streets, winters, and self-possession.

High Ground is part of Pawn Island Records, the independent label and release-world home founded by Matthew H. Freeman.
