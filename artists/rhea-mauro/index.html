Rhea Mauro

Soulful belting / adult contemporary / country

Rhea Mauro is one of the most flexible corners of the catalog. The songs can belt, confide, or loosen into something playful depending on where the writing wants to go.

Rhea Mauro is part of Pawn Island Records, the independent label and release-world home founded by Matthew H. Freeman.
