Project Identity

World and Sound

Resunant is Pawn Island Records' alternative power metal and narrative-driven glam rock project. It should be framed less as traditional fantasy metal and more as a theatrical, story-heavy, high-voltage rock world.

The project lives where metal force meets glam-rock drama and sci-fi or comic-book scale: heavy guitars, big choruses, neon atmosphere, character, spectacle, and concept-album energy.

It is heavy, but not grim. The project should feel electric, visual, and mythic in a modern rock sense: a midnight stage, a revival show, a neon sermon, a poster from another timeline.

Story Angles

  • Alternative power metal fused with narrative-driven glam rock.
  • Midnight Revival as a theatrical, concept-scale rock world.
  • Heavy music with glam drama, neon atmosphere, and story-forward presentation.
