Platform Vision

What Release Deck is being built to do

The goal is to put strategy, timing, audience signals, and campaign execution in one clear operating view so smaller teams can move with major-label precision.

Release intelligence

Track active releases, catalog priorities, platform links, rollout milestones, and the moving pieces that define an effective campaign window.

Audience analytics

Turn platform performance into usable context so artists and label teams can see what is gaining traction and respond with smarter next moves.

Marketing command

Coordinate campaigns, outreach, content planning, and release-specific actions inside a focused workspace built for modern independent music teams.
