Quiet Filter Press Kit

Quiet Filter is Pawn Island Records' stoner doom and grunge-groove project. Blues-stained, hypnotic, gritty, and heavy with reverb, the project lives in a world of worn surfaces, dirty light, circular riffs, and spiritual unease.

For press, playlist, booking, or business inquiries, contact Pawn Island Records directly at pawnisland@outlook.com.
