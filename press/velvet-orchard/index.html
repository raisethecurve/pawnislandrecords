Velvet Orchard Press Kit

Velvet Orchard is Pawn Island Records' experimental electronic-pop project. Beginning in bubblegum Y2K pop, it has evolved through EDM, house, dubstep, bass, club, and festival-inspired electronica with a restless, high-gloss sense of experimentation.

For press, playlist, booking, or business inquiries, contact Pawn Island Records directly at pawnisland@outlook.com.
