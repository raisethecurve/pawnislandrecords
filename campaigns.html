Landing Variants

Different releases may need different lead-ins.

Pre-save campaigns might prioritize urgency and action, while catalog titles may perform better with video, lyrics, or a fuller world-building frame.

Testing Notes

Use one idea per page instead of asking every landing page to do everything.

Strong campaign pages usually lead with one clear promise, then support it with the right asset: video, story, press framing, merch, or direct ownership.
